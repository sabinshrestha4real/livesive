Introduction

Welcome to Livesive ("we", "our", or "us"). Livesive is a video streaming and e-commerce platform that connects users through live content and online shopping experiences.

We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the app.

Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account or use our services, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile photo

2.2 Information from Device Permissions

With your explicit consent, we collect:

PermissionPurpose
Camera To enable live video streaming and capture photos for listings or profile
Photo Library To allow uploading images for products, profile photos, or posts
Notifications To send you order updates, live stream alerts, and promotional messages

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Enable live video streaming features
  • Process e-commerce transactions and orders
  • Send push notifications for order status, streams, and promotions
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Respond to customer support inquiries
  • Detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access

Third-Party Services

Livesive uses the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

  • Firebase (Google) — Analytics, authentication, cloud messaging, and crash reporting
  • Payment Processors — For secure handling of e-commerce transactions

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for camera, photo library, or notification permissions at any time via your device settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Camera & Photo Library

We access your camera and photo library only when you actively use features that require them (e.g., starting a live stream or uploading a product image). We do not access your camera or photos in the background.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • A push notification in the app
  • A prominent notice on our app or website
  • An email to your registered address

Continued use of Livesive after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
